Optical Character Recognition

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is a text based recognition and extraction method allowing the user extract handwritten, printed or generated text in images, such as posters, street signs and product labels, or from documents like articles, reports, forms, and invoices. This is typically extracted as words, lines, paragraphs or text blocks. Read more here. OCR is the underlying technique behind Applied AI services such as Azure Form Recognizer which extracts data from documents.
