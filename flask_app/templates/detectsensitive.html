Computer Vision models like the one presented in this demo can be trained to detect adult material in images so developers can restrict the display of these images in their software. Scores are applied between zero and one (or converted into a percentage) so developers can interpret the results according to their own preferences. For example, anything under 20% gore can be displayed. Adult images are explicitly sexual, Racy images are sexually suggestive and Gory images show blood/gore. Read more here.
