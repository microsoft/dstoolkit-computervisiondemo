Microsoft Computer Vision Demo

Computer Vision is a subfield of Artificial Intelligence which enables machines and computers to gain insight, infomation or knowlege from images or videos. It is used extensively across various industries such as energy, public sector, manufacturing, retail, automotive and more. Tasks are varied and cover a variety of uses. This demo website hosts a variety of these use cases, both Azure based and custom Computer Vision models are demoed. Each Computer Vision technique can be applied to videos aswell using each frame as an image. See below for simple examples and use the navigation panel on the left to navigate between the pages where you can upload your own images* and see the power of Computer Vision first hand.

Read more about Microsoft Computer Vision documentation and SDKs here, Microsoft Computer Vision offerings here and Microsoft Custom Vision offerings here.
